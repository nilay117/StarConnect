Everyone loves great art but who cares about great artists? We do.

We are a multi - stakeholder network of upcoming artists, celebrities, fans and NGOs.

How it works?

We take content from promising musicians, showcase it on our platform and get the best video shared by celebrities from their Facebook pages. We then donate all money collected from registered artists to our partner NGO, which then acknowledges the celebrity’s contribution on Social Media.
