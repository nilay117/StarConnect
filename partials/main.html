Everyone loves great art but who cares about great artists? We do.

We are a multi - stakeholder network of upcoming artists, celebrities, fans and NGOs.

How it works?

We take content from promising musicians, showcase it on our platform and get the best video shared by celebrities from their Facebook pages. We then donate all money collected from registered artists to our partner NGO, which then acknowledges the celebrity’s contribution on Social Media.

  • Artists

    If you’re an artist, this is your opportunity to get your work publicly shared by top music celebrities and showcase your content to a niche community of music fans

  • Celebrities

    Music celebrities, your support helps discover and promote hidden talent while contributing to a social cause at the same time.

  • NGOs

    Use funds donated by us to boost social projects and benefit the society at large.

  • Exposure

    This self - sustaining cycle discovers new talent and empowers social causes through celebrities who care.
