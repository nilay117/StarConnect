Dilli Beats

At Star Connect, it’s our mission to discover hidden talent and we believe that the search should start right where the youth resides. Star Connect in association with Namyoho Studios proudly presents Dilli Beats: an Online College Music Competition across 50 Delhi Colleges wherein the top 15 musicians get professional music videos and audio recordings each shot by Namyoho Studios for free. They then get their work reviewed by the likes of Parikrama, Thaikkudam Bridge, Swarathma and Euphoria and finally promoted by them through their social media handles (for a minimum of 4 artists)! What else? One lucky artist also gets the opportunity to go live on Facebook with Parikrama! Moreover, the registration fees will be used to donate a combined sum of Rs, 50,000 to the Smile Foundation to help underprivileged children.

How it Works?

Register with a video link of your past singing performance, get maximum engagement on your video to be a part of the top 30. After shortlisting of the top 15 from the top 30 by Namyoho Studios and Star Connect, free professional music videos will be recorded for the top 15 and these would be sent to celebrities for review. The top 4 would be promoted by celebrities.

Registrations: Fees - Rs. 250. All money collected from registrations will be used to donate a combined sum of Rs. 50,000 to the Smile Foundation to help underprivileged children. Registrations open till 5th October.

Competition: Get maximum points (views + engagement) on your video to win! Dates - 15th October - 25th October. More details to follow.
